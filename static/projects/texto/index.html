Vapourwave

A Vapourwave style effect, using PaperJS and CSS.

The PaperJS text animation is over a background image, with a noise .gif applied on hover.

A Figure-8 path and continuous rotation give the feeling of random movement.

Pastel

Inspired by the Tumblr ' a e s t h e t i c ', this squishy grid is powered by PaperJS.

Vectors are used to move the grid closer to the cursor on hover, proportional to the distance.

Move your mouse over the grid to see the effect.
